# Slime Slayer 
My first game built in Godot 4 as a beginner.

## How to Play
- Arrow keys/WASD to move
- Space to jump
- Collect all 10 coins to win
- Avoid slimes or you die!

## Built With
- Godot 4
- GDScript

## What I Learned
- 2D platformer mechanics
- Tilemaps and world building
- Signals and scene management
- Collision layers and masks
